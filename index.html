About us

We are an emotional support group for young people with personality problems and who suffer from abandonment and tend to live at home to have a high self-esteem and try to overcome the problems that arise in adolescence.

Objective

1Objetive

It is to help young people with different problems that arise in adolescence, who have had various difficulties in their family environment, and have suffered from abandonment, we will help them through various talks which will help them to function outside their environment in the which should be adapted.

2Mission

transform the mentality of young people to give them peace of mind and teach them that they are not alone in the world, that they have the support of our foundation where we will give them the emotional foundations and help them to trust them again and feel safe in any environment with the adaptability.
